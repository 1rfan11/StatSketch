FAQ

Is the free version time-limited?

No. The Community Edition is free forever. No trials, no hidden timers.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Art XP is Local-First. It requires no internet connection to function.

How do updates work?

The app auto-updates. New tailored exercises and features are rolled out to everyone.

Can I customize the exercises?

Yes. You can add your own custom reference folders and configure timer durations.

Is my data private?

Yes. Everything is stored locally on your device. We cannot see your stats, streaks, or artwork.
