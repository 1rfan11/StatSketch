Frequently Asked Questions

Is the free version time-limited?

No. The Evaluation version is free forever. You can use it as long as you like. The Pro license (coming soon) is for those who want to support development and remove the occasional reminder popups.

Do I need a drawing tablet?

It is recommended for the drawing exercises, but you can navigate the app and use the timers with a mouse and keyboard.

Does it work offline?

Yes. Art XP is Local-First software. It works 100% offline and stores all your data on your machine.

How do updates work?

The app will check for updates automatically on launch. Major updates (like new features in the Pro tier) will be optional, but bug fixes and new exercises are delivered free to everyone.
