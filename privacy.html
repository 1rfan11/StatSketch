Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Statsketch ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our desktop application.

Statsketch is a local-first desktop application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, drawings, or practice usage to any external servers.
