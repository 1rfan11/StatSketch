Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

1. Introduction

StatSketch ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our desktop application.

2. Data Collection

StatSketch is a local-first desktop application. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, drawings, or practice usage to any external servers.

  • Local Storage: All your settings, stats, and preferences are stored locally on your device.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or third-party analytics in the desktop app.
  • No Account Required: StatSketch works completely offline without requiring registration or login.

3. Your Data Stays Yours

Everything you create and all progress you make in StatSketch remains on your computer. We have no access to your practice data, drawings, or any information about how you use the application.

4. Contact

If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us via our support channels.
